Mercari Price Suggestion

Mercari is a U.S. online marketplace where millions of people buy and sell almost anything — giving unused and outgrown items a second life.

This tool is a solution to the Mercari Price Suggestion Challenge — a Kaggle competition to automatically suggest the right price to sellers based on their product's title, description, brand, category, and condition. (The training data used here is quite old, so predicted prices might not be relevant with current times)
